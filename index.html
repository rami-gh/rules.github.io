Server Rules and Guidelines

  • 1. Be Respectful

    Treat all members with kindness and respect. Do not engage in any form of harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks. Everyone deserves to feel welcome and valued.

  • 2. Stay On Topic

    Keep discussions relevant to the channel's topic. Off-topic conversations should be moved to the appropriate channels to maintain organization.

  • 3. No Self-Promotion

    Avoid excessive self-promotion or spamming. Sharing your work is encouraged, but please ensure it is done in moderation and in the relevant channels dedicated to promotions or media sharing.

  • 4. Helpful and Constructive

    When providing feedback or assistance, always aim to be constructive and helpful. Focus on the content or issue, not the person. Criticism should be delivered in a friendly manner.

  • 5. No NSFW Content

    This is a family-friendly community. All content, including links, discussions, and uploaded media, must be safe-for-work and appropriate for all ages. NSFW content will be removed immediately.

  • 6. Respect Privacy

    Do not share any personal information, either your own or that of others. Privacy is a fundamental right for all members and must be respected at all times.

  • 7. Use Appropriate Channels

    Please post in the appropriate channels based on the content or topic of discussion. Always read the channel descriptions for guidance on where specific discussions should take place.

  • 8. No Spoilers

    If you wish to discuss content that may contain spoilers (e.g., TV shows, movies, games), use spoiler tags or dedicate specific channels for spoilers to avoid ruining the experience for others.

  • 9. Follow Discord Terms of Service

    You must adhere to Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. Any violations will be taken seriously and may result in disciplinary action, including warnings, kicks, or bans.

  • 10. Listen to Moderators

    Moderators are here to ensure the community is enjoyable and safe. Always follow their instructions. Disrespecting moderators will not be tolerated.

  • 11. Report Issues

    If you encounter any issues or observe rule violations, report them to the moderators directly. Do not take matters into your own hands. We work together to resolve issues efficiently.

  • 12. Be Inclusive

    This is an inclusive community where everyone is welcome. Avoid discriminatory behavior based on race, gender, nationality, sexual orientation, or religion.

  • 13. Avoid Toxic Behavior

    Toxicity will not be tolerated. We want to build a friendly and welcoming environment where everyone can feel safe. If you're being overly negative, please reconsider your approach.

  • 14. No Impersonation

    Do not impersonate other members, moderators, or server staff. This includes using similar names, profile pictures, or other tactics to confuse others.

Failure to comply with these rules may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans at the discretion of the moderators. Please remember that this community thrives on mutual respect, collaboration, and positivity.

Thank you for being a part of our community! Let's work together to create an enjoyable environment for everyone.
